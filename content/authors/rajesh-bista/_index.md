Mr. [Rajesh Bista](https://www.researchgate.net/profile/Rajesh-Bista) is a Doctoral Candidate in the Department of Geography at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. 
He used socio-economic data from household surveys to examine land use change under the impacts of forest policy.
<br>

His recent publications include:
- [Cropland abandonment in community forestry in Nepal](https://www.qzgeog.com/publication/p2021-bista-abandon/)
  published in [_Earth Interactions_](https://www.ametsoc.org/index.cfm/ams/publications/journals/earth-interactions/) in 2021
